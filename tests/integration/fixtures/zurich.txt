The city of Zurich is the largest city in Switzerland. It is located in the north of the country, at the northwestern tip of Lake Zurich. With a population of approximately 430,000, it is the most populous city in Switzerland.

Zurich is a leading global city and among the world's largest financial centers. The city is home to a large number of financial institutions and banking firms. Zurich's main railway station, Zurich HB, is the largest train station in Switzerland and one of the busiest in Europe.

The city is known for its high quality of life and has consistently ranked as one of the most livable cities in the world. Zurich has a rich cultural scene with numerous museums, galleries, and theaters. The Zurich Opera House and the Kunsthaus Zurich art museum are major cultural landmarks.

The old town of Zurich, known as the Altstadt, sits on both sides of the Limmat River. The area features preserved medieval architecture and narrow cobblestone streets.
